Paintless Dent Repair

The original factory paint on most standard vehicles is flexible and can often be repaired without painting. Auto Collision Repaired uses best practices when using these methods to bring your vehicle to pre-accident condition.

Cracked or Broken Glass

Whether your windshield needs a complete replacement or is cracked or shattered, bring your vehicle to the experts at Auto Collision Repaired.

Car Bumper and Car Fender

At Auto Collision Repaired, our team meticulously reconditions each bumper to erase any signs of dents, scratches, scrapes, or indentations. Our skilled technicians carefully blend the appended paint more accurately to achieve a pre-accident appearance.

New Paint or Spot Paint

Paint chips on your vehicle can result from thrown up rocks, inclement weather, or from a collision. They’re often hard to cover up with simple touch-up paint and can detract from the appearance of your vehicle. Luckily, the team at Auto Collision Repaired employs the finest painters to get your vehicle’s paint finish looking like new again.
